## 📖 About Me

<div style="background: linear-gradient(135deg, #0a0e27 0%, #1a1f3a 100%); padding: 20px; border-radius: 10px; border: 1px solid #00D9FF; box-shadow: 0 0 20px rgba(0, 217, 255, 0.3);">

I am a **Master's student in Autonomous Systems and Robotics** at [Technische Universität Darmstadt](https://www.tu-darmstadt.de/), where I focus on **intelligent robotics systems, SLAM, computer vision, and Transformer methods** for scene understanding and task planning. I work on end-to-end robotics systems that combine perception, planning, and control.

I have extensive experience in deploying ML models on edge devices and integrating complex robotic systems. In my **real-time navigation demonstration (TUD, 2025)**, I independently completed the integration of perception and control systems, achieving stable operation and performance optimization for autonomous exploration tasks.

My research interests focus on **perception fusion and environmental interaction in intelligent robotics systems**, particularly applying **SLAM, visual servoing, and Transformer** methods to scene understanding, action recognition, and task planning. I am looking forward to working in the fields of wearable robotics, rehabilitation, and therapeutic robots.

**Languages:** 🇨🇳 Chinese (Native) | 🇬🇧 English (Fluent) | 🇩🇪 German (TestDaF 16) | 🇯🇵 Japanese (Basic)
